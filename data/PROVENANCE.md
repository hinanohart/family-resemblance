Files:
- `5740-t.tex` — canonical LaTeX source as published by Project Gutenberg (binary-identical to upstream).
- `tractatus.txt` — regex-stripped plain-text approximation derived from the `.tex`. The conversion is intentionally lossy and is **not** authoritative; cite `5740-t.tex` for any reproduction. See `tractatus_index.json` for SHA-256 of both files.

Notes:
- The Ogden translation was first published in 1922 and is in the public domain in the United States. Users outside the US should confirm the status under their local copyright law before redistribution.
- The Project Gutenberg header/footer is part of the released eBook and is retained in both files.
- This project's clustering and `[mcp]` modules do not depend on the Tractatus text at runtime; the corpus is included only as a textual reference for documentation and example notebooks (Phase 5+).
